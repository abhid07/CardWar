Card War

  • Instructions

    • Priority of cards from higher to lower :

    1. Click on 'View Card' button to see your card

    2. Click on 'Bet Button' to bet your chips

    3. 3 bets are compulsory to do

    4. After 3 bets you can click on 'Show' button to see the result or you can continue your bet

    5. The player who has higher priority card will win and get all the betted chips

    6. Process will continue until one of the player loses his all chips
